"Every battle is won before it's ever fought." - Sun Tzu



​



Most people prepare for tough conversations by rehearsing in the

shower. Then someone says something unexpected and their entire

game plan evaporates.



​



There's a better way. It's called SPAR, and it turns AI into your

sparring partner before the real conversation happens.



​



S - Sharpen. Have AI interview you (one question at a time) until

you're crystal clear on what you want, what you'll settle for,

and what makes you walk away.



​



P - Probe. Feed your position to a fresh AI and ask it to

steelman the other side's best argument. Then ask for the five

most likely reactions to your points.



​



A - Attack. Switch AI from friendly coach to hardcore critic.

Find every hole in your argument before someone else does.



​



R - Rehearse. Open voice mode. Have the actual conversation with

AI. Do it three to six times at different intensity levels.



​



By the time you walk into that room, you've already had the

conversation five times. Nothing catches you off guard.
