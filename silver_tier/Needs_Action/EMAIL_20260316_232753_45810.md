"A jack of all trades is a master of none, but oftentimes better

than a master of one."



​



Every business owner faces situations they know nothing about.

Hiring for a new role. Evaluating software. Working with an

agency for the first time.



​



Most people open ChatGPT and ask one generic question. Generic

question, generic answer, generic results.



​



Instead, try three prompts in the same conversation. I call it

Map, Mind, Move.



​



Map: "I'm a [role] facing [situation]. Give me a 5-minute

executive briefing. Key terms, major options, and what outsiders

consistently get wrong."



​



Mind: "Now go deeper. What seems simple but is actually tricky?

What are the most expensive mistakes people like me make?"



​



Move: "Based on everything we discussed, give me the 3-5

questions I should ask, the smartest first step, and red flags to

watch for."



​



Same conversation. Each phase builds on the last. You walk in

knowing the vocabulary, the traps, and exactly what to do first.
