Harvard Online

 



 Learn to lead from any position.



 Created by Harvard Kennedy School faculty Ronald Heifetz, Adaptive Leadership teaches a powerful, well-tested framework to help you lead more effectively in times of complexity and change. Going beyond theory, this course features the stories of former students—people from all walks of life and levels of experience—who’ve used these tools to make a real difference in their organizations and communities.



 



 Through their journeys, along with interactive exercises and insights from changemakers across sectors, you’ll learn how to:



 

  Lead with and beyond your authority

  Mobilize people to tackle tough challenges

  Create lasting impact—even when the path forward is unclear
