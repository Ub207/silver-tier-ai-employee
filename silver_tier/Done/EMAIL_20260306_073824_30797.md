###⚡️ **Lightning Editor Engine**





We want every action in RemNote to feel instant (0.1 seconds or less). We’re one step closer to that goal with our new “lightning” editor rendering engine:





- ****Fast Document Loading: All documents should now load faster, in a single pass. **** 

- **Fluid Rendering and Editing: **Our new engine renders much faster, helping you to stay in flow while working. 

- **Smooth Mobile Scrolling:  **No more jittering! 

- **Improved Interactions and Animations: **We’ve polished every corner of the editor. Zooming, sticky ancestors, and animations will feel particularly improved.





[ **Jump Back Into RemNote**](http://r.newsletter.remnote.com/mk/cl/f/sh/WCPzyXJTZ6uvcxCbWDmNhweJiBJqTlBM/EZjGSh8jT9Sy)





###**🪧 New Slash Menu**

We’ve revamped the slash menu, making it cleaner and more compact.





###**💬 Full App Translations**

We’ve comprehensively translated the entire app. Translations are currently available for Brazilian Portuguese, Chinese, German, Polish, and Russian, with more coming soon.





Enable translations in *Settings > Language > Interface Language.*





We’ve also added a new “Chinese Search Mode” to the desktop app, which fixes many issues with Chinese text not being reliably findable. Enable from *Settings > Desktop App > Search Tokenizer*.





Let us know what languages you want to see next [on the feedback forum](http://r.newsletter.remnote.com/mk/cl/f/sh/WCPzyXJTZ72igzPrsJG3Tgo6qyCxjSnU/Fd2-cpUFnS7H)!
