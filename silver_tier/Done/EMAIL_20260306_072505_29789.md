Most people use GPT-5 like a simple chatbot. They throw in a quick question, get an answer, and move on. But here’s the truth GPT-5 isn’t just a Q&A tool—it’s an analyst, strategist, editor, coach, and even a planner if you know how to prompt it right. This guide gives you 20 practical prompts (with real-world use cases) to unlock GPT-5’s full power. Why Prompts Matter GPT-5 adapts to the way you ask. Think of prompts as the steering wheel—the difference between a random response and a structured, usable answer.
