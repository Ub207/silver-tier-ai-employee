Hey builders,



This week’s update is all about keeping things clean and efficient. We just shipped “/clear”, a simple command that helps you reset, save tokens, and stay focused:



Give it a /clear



We just shipped a smarter way to keep your workspace lean: you can now instantly clear the conversation history when you're done with a task and want a clean slate. This helps to keep your context focused and reduces costs by not carrying unnecessary messages into your next prompts. Simply type "/clear" into the prompt box.



Give it a try, we know you’ll enjoy it.
