## Root Cause



The jittering/skipping sound you're experiencing is caused by **insufficient audio buffering**. The Twilio example was sending very small audio chunks (often just a few bytes) directly to Twilio as they arrived from OpenAI, which caused choppy/jittery playback.



## Solution



PR #1926 implements **symmetrical buffering** for outgoing audio (OpenAI → Twilio), matching the existing 50ms buffering strategy for incoming audio (Twilio → OpenAI):



- Buffers small audio chunks from OpenAI before sending to Twilio

- Flushes when buffer reaches 50ms worth of audio (400 bytes at 8kHz)

- Includes periodic flush for any remaining buffered data
