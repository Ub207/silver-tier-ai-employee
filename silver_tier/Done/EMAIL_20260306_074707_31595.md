###**🎉 RemNote 1.21 - Lecture Recorder, Tabs, New Study Mode and more!**



[ **Try the Lecture Recorder**](http://r.newsletter.remnote.com/mk/cl/f/sh/WCPzyXJTZ6uvcxCbWDmNhweJiBJqTlBM/1j5Pa_9xPGhu)



###**🎤 Lecture Recorder**





Record audio from lectures and view a live transcript!





**Bullets View**

Switch to “Bullets” for an organized outline of the key ideas in your lecture.





**Notes Linked to Audio Timestamps**

Notes taken during a lecture are automatically linked to the corresponding timestamp in your audio.





**AI-Generated Flashcards, Quizzes, and Summaries**



Immediately generate learning and study materials from any lecture transcript.





###**🏠 **Tabs****





RemNote now supports tabbed navigation (like a web browser). Each tab can contain one or more panes and stays open until closed.





[ **Enable Tabs**](http://r.newsletter.remnote.com/mk/cl/f/sh/WCPzyXJTZ72igzPrsJG3Tgo6qyCxjSnU/34QcUaqYHHm4)





###🧠 **Study Without Recording Answer Choices**





You can now study documents or folders without recording your answer choices. This lets you practice without impacting your flashcards’ spaced-repetition history – useful if you want to preview newly created cards or let a friend test your flashcards.





[ **Jump Back into RemNote**](http://r.newsletter.remnote.com/mk/cl/f/sh/WCPzyXJTZ7AVl1d8EOjjFQxtzl64zAPc/DmDgbDLGKQ92)





RemNote 1.21 also includes tons of smaller polish and bug-fixes. Notable improvements include 10x faster PDF-to-text conversion, a “Card Correctness” insight, smoother PDF scrolling, and more!



 



Thank you for helping us to build the world’s best tool for learning and thinking! Even more is on the way in the coming weeks 👀



Happy learning!
