I agree with @seratch that having additional Session implementations in the repo could complicate releases and incur an additional maintainability overhead. At the very least, I think it's worth being very conservative in the Session implementations added. `SQLAlchemySession`, `RedisSession`, and `DjangoSession` would cover a lot of bases. I applaud the work on #1163 but I think it would be more appropriate to have a generic `SQLAlchemySession` that would allow users to choose between the relational database of their choosing, not just Postgres.
