****📱New Phone App****

======================





**Fast Startup**



The app starts up ~10x faster (in less than half a second on our test devices).





**New Home Screen**



Instantly quick-capture thoughts, view your docs, jump into flashcards, and more.





**Instant Practicing**



Immediately begin practicing flashcards from the “Practice” button on the new home screen. No waiting!





**New Mobile PDF Reader**



We've cleaned up the toolbars, expanded your reading area, polished menus, and more!





**Streak Widgets**



Build a practice habit by installing the “Streak” widget to your home screen. Tapping brings you to your flashcards in less than half a second. Take advantage of downtime while traveling or waiting in line.





**New Menus**



Enjoy cleaner menus throughout the app.





**New Folder & Document UI**



Easier-to-use quick actions and a tidier feel.





******Offline **PDFs on Mobile****



PDFs are now accessible offline on the mobile app. (PDFs are already always available offline on the desktop app.)





... and many more mobile improvements!
