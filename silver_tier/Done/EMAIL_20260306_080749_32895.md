Today’s email is for anyone who wants to make real money from bug

bounties in 2025.

Now, if you’re new to this space…



Bug bounties are programs where companies pay hackers to find and

report security bugs before someone bad does.



You find a flaw, report it responsibly, and get paid for helping

them stay secure.



Simple idea, but how you actually win at it has changed a lot…



Recent reports from 2024 and 2025 show that the “generalist” era

is over.



The hunters who are making consistent money now are the ones who

go deep in a specific niche, but not just any niche…



They choose areas where the payouts are going up and the

duplicate rates are going down.



So it’s not just about being a specialist anymore…



It’s about knowing where to focus, what tools to use, and how to

hunt smarter than everyone else.



Here’s the playbook you can try for yourself:



1) Pick a niche that actually pays

----------------------------------



Don’t just pick “mobile” or “web.” Go where the money and

low-duplicate programs are.



* Smart contracts / L2 rollups: On-chain logic and formal tools

still pay massive top rewards.

​

* APIs: Fintech and SaaS APIs are goldmines for BOLA, OAuth, and

shadow API bugs.

​

* Apple ecosystem: Chaining sandbox escapes or Lockdown Mode bugs

can hit $2M payouts this year.



When choosing, look for three things: big recent payouts, active

programs, and low duplicate chatter.



2) Build a realistic lab

------------------------



Forget random CTFs. Create a setup that feels like a real

program.



* API track: Use OpenAPI specs, Postman, and tools to test rate

limits and token logic.

​

* Smart contract track: Use Foundry, Slither, and small scripts

to test reentrancy or state logic.

​

* Apple track: Try WebKit builds, sandbox
