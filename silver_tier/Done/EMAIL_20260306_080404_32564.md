 Real life looks like this: checking progress on a plane before takeoff, 
kicking off a run on the train, nudging your build between meetings in a 
rideshare, catching up on your commute, or giving a quick review while you’re 
waiting in line at a concert or restaurant. Little moments add up to shipped 
apps.

Log into the mobile app so you can:


 * See progress at a glance. Peek at what your Agent’s doing right from your 
lock screen (live activities on iOS), so you always know what’s moving forward.
 * Make quick moves between moments. Create a new app, review results, or give 
a quick nudge in seconds (simple in-app controls).
 * Keep the flow going across devices. Open on your phone, continue on your 
laptop—everything stays in sync with your Replit account.
 * Jump back in fast. Recent projects and lightweight views make it easy to 
pick up the thread and keep momentum.
