****1. Start Your Day with a To-Do List****



Begin your day with a list of tasks. This list serves as your map for the rest of the day. Without this map, it's easy to get lost in thousands of lines of code, and by the end of the day, you might wonder what you've been doing and what you've achieved, leaving you feeling unproductive.



****2. Manage Your Daily and Long-Term Tasks****



Write down your daily tasks in a notebook. After completing each task, put a tick next to it. This gives you a great sense of reward, like you've accomplished something.
