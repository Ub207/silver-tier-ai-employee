###**📃 Text Reader Mode for PDFs**





You can now view any PDF in “Text Mode” for easy, distraction-free reading. This converts PDFs from a rigid fixed-size printout into a resizable text document, letting you adjust font sizes, read on mobile, use text-to-speech, and more. All text, images, formulas, tables, and so on are maintained.





[ **Try the Text Reader**](http://r.newsletter.remnote.com/mk/cl/f/sh/WCPzyXJTZ6uvcxCbWDmNhweJiBJqTlBM/1YGA6n3BjcVL)





**Easy Reading on Phones**



Text nicely wraps on mobile screens. No need to squint trying to read small fonts on PDFs.





**Customizable Appearance**



Customize your document’s size and font to fit perfectly on your screen.





**Keyboard Navigation**



Easily navigate up/down with your keyboard while reading.



**Text to Speech**



Listen to your documents with high-quality text-to-speech.
