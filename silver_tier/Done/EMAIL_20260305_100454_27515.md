Hi, thanks for asking the question. As you noticed, the sessions feature is not supported in Realtime Agents. The only approach I can suggest at this moment is to receive events and serialize those in your datastore like [this example does](https://github.com/openai/openai-agents-python/blob/v0.2.6/examples/realtime/app/server.py#L77-L117). The example does not handle transcript text data, but there is nothing to prevent your app from storing the data too.
