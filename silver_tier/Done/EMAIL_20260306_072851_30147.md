Last month brought two powerful releases, 1.48 and 1.49, focused on giving you more creative control and fine-tuned detail in your apps. Now you can design dynamic layouts with flex containers, render PDFs natively without any workarounds, and tell richer data stories with sparklines and dataframe cell selections. Dive into the details below, learn about a new GenAI course, and hear the latest from the community.

&#128680; Release 1.48 + 1.49

1.48 Highlights 







	- &#8596; Announcing horizontal flex containers! Configure the alignment, direction, and gap of containers to create dynamic layouts.

	- &#129717; st.logout improvements will log a user out of their identity provider for a more secure session. 

	- &#128172; Configure the dismissibility of st.dialog and set callback functions for more control.





1.49 Highlights 







	- &#128196; Introducing st.pdf! Render PDF documents natively right in your app.

	- &#128296; Dataframes now support cell selections for more precise and interactive data analysis

	- &#10024; Add sparklines to st.metric to show trends at a glance.
