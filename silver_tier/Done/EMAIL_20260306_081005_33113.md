Hi, thanks for reaching out! I understand this can be a bit confusing, but these voices aren't supported in the Realtime API—they're only available in the Audio Speech API.



>The voice the model uses to respond. Voice cannot be changed during the session once the model has responded with audio at least once. Current voice options are alloy, ash, ballad, coral, echo, sage, shimmer, verse, marin, and cedar. We recommend 

marin and cedar for best quality.



The outcome occurs because `session.update` fails, as these voices aren't supported. As a result, the output doesn't follow the expected format. If you check the events, the ones related to `session.update` should be failing.



References:

- Realtime: https://platform.openai.com/docs/api-reference/realtime/create-call#realtime-create-call-session-audio-output-voice

- Audio Speech: https://platform.openai.com/docs/api-reference/audio/createSpeech#audio-createspeech-voice
