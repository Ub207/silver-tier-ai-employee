Langfuse has three core pillars:

 * Observability – trace every LLM call, chain steps and cost on one timeline.



 * Prompt management – version‑control prompts and roll them out without redeploys.



 * Evaluations – auto‑ or human‑grade results once you have traffic to benchmark.
