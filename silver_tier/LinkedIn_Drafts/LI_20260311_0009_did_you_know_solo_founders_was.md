**Did you know solo founders waste 30% of their time on admin work?**

As a solo founder, you wear many hats. But let's be honest, managing admin work is a significant time suck. The truth is, 1 in 3 solo founders spend more than 30% of their time on non-core activities.

The problem is, when you're not focused on growing your business, you're not growing your business. You're stuck in a cycle of "survival mode" instead of "growth mode." 

That's why it's essential to prioritize founder productivity. By automating repetitive tasks and focusing on high-leverage activities, you can free up time and energy to focus on what matters most - your business.

**What's the one thing you're currently wasting time on that could be automated? Share with me in the comments below!**
